I am an applied microeconomist with research interests in labor economics, development economics, and the economics of education. I earned a Ph.D. in Economics from the University of Illinois and I currently work as a Senior Associate in the Labor & Employment practice at Charles River Associates. I originally hail from Benin, grew up in its most vibrant city, Cotonou, but also lived in various West African cities such as Lomé, Accra, and Ouagadougou.
